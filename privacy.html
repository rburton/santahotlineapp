Privacy Policy

App: Santa Hotline (iOS)

Effective date: November 24, 2025

Summary

Santa Hotline is designed for families and children and places privacy and safety first. The app does not collect, store, or share personal data. There are no ads, no analytics, no tracking technologies, no cookies, and no third‑party SDKs that profile users.

The app requests access to your camera and microphone solely to show a live, on‑device preview during the simulated call experience. Nothing is recorded, saved, transcribed, or transmitted—the video and audio remain on your device and disappear as soon as you close the experience.

What the app does

The app provides short, pre‑recorded, festive video call experiences with characters like Santa, Mrs. Claus, elves, a snowman, and a reindeer, plus a seasonal countdown. All experiences run entirely on‑device.

Data we do not collect

  • No personal information (such as names, emails, phone numbers, ages, or addresses).
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV), IP addresses, or precise location.
  • No usage analytics or diagnostics from users.
  • No saved photos, videos, audio recordings, or contacts. Live camera/microphone input is processed only in real time on-device and is never stored.
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Device access and permissions

Santa Hotline requests access to the camera and microphone so it can display your live video and audio locally during the simulated call. This access is used only while the feature is active. No frames or audio are recorded, saved, uploaded, or shared. If you prefer, you can deny these permissions and continue to enjoy the pre‑recorded experiences without the live preview.

The app does not request access to contacts, photos, files, precise location, or other sensitive data.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for children and complies with children’s privacy principles by avoiding collection of personal data. Because we do not collect any information, there is nothing to store, use, or share about a child’s identity or behavior.

Third‑party services

The app does not integrate third‑party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or social plugins. No data is sent to external services for profiling or marketing.

Data storage and retention

We do not maintain servers for this app and do not store user data on a backend. The app does not create cloud backups of any personal information. Consequently, there is no data to retain or delete.

Your choices

As the app does not collect any personal data, there are no settings required to limit data collection. You may delete the app at any time.

International use

The app functions the same in all regions. Since no personal data is collected, there is no cross‑border data transfer.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this page and modify the effective date above. Because the app collects no data, changes are expected to be minimal and focused on clarity.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, please reach out using the contact information provided on the Santa Hotline App Store listing or the developer’s support channel.
